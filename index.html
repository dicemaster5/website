Games I've worked on

Townbox

A single player sandbox town building game with an emphasis on creativity and self expression! Take on the role of trainee town planner and expand your teeny-tiny starting island into a bustling town of your own design.

Sonic Forces: Speed Battles

A casual multiplayer racing & battle game from SEGA! Run, race, and compete in multiplayer running games with real players from around the world!

Planetary Pest Control

An asymmetrical 2-player couch co-op action game where you control two unique characters, each with distinctive abilities and synergies. Play across 6 levels, offering up to 2 hours of gameplay, all set on a vibrant fungal planet brought to life in a striking comic book style.

Zephyr: The Cloud Odyssey

A first-person speedrunning platformer where players race across floating islands, dodging deadly traps to collect artifact pieces as quickly as possible.

Lost To Be Found

A short relaxing treasure hunting game made for the 2021 Global Game Jam. Made in a team of 3 at the Spelkollektivet game dev house. You can play it in your browser right now!
